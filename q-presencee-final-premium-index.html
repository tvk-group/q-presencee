Q-Presence

A quantum-ready proof-of-presence and identity verification layer for sovereign digital ecosystems.

Q-Presence is the biometric and contextual proof-of-presence module of the SOVRA / Entelekron ecosystem. It is designed to verify whether a person, wallet, device, validator, or authorized participant is genuinely present, active, and permitted to perform a sensitive action.

Q-Presence is a security and identity infrastructure concept. Biometric, KYC, access-control, and quantum-resistant features require strict legal, privacy, consent, and regulatory review before production deployment.

How It Works

From identity signal to permissioned action.

Q-Presence follows a structured verification flow: identify, validate, bind, seal, and authorize.

Step 01

Identify

A user, wallet, validator, device, or participant requests access or performs a sensitive action.

Step 02

Validate

Biometric, device, location-context, session, or wallet-based signals are checked according to policy.

Step 03

Seal

The verification event is timestamped and connected to ChronoSeal or GraphVAULT for auditability.

Step 04

Authorize

The system grants or denies access based on verified presence, role, permission, and risk context.
